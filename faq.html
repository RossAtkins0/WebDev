Frequently Asked Questions

What types of events do you cater for?

We cater for a variety of events, including weddings, corporate functions, private parties, and more. Contact us to discuss your specific requirements!

How can I place an order?

You can place an order by contacting us directly via phone or email. Alternatively, you can fill out the order form on our website.

Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?

Yes, we offer options for various dietary restrictions including vegetarian, vegan, gluten-free, and more. Please inform us of any dietary requirements when placing your order.

Can I make changes to my order after it's been placed?

Changes to orders may be accommodated depending on availability and timing. Please contact us as soon as possible if you need to make changes to your order.

What is your cancellation policy?

Our cancellation policy varies depending on the type of event and the timing of the cancellation. Please refer to our terms and conditions or contact us for more information.
